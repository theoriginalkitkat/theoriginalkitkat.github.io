Katherine Scott

Design Innovation Specialist


Katherine is an inventor, designer, consultant, and avid researcher of technology, fashion and mass customization of apparel and footwear. Her work in 3D printing and digital fabrication includes prototyping footwear, apparel, 3D printing on fabric, auxetic structures and experimental textiles.

International
Clean Energy Summit
2018

Presented By: Bird News Climate Alliance

BCA International Clean Energy summit provides networking opportunities for public administrations, companies, social entrepreneurs, development partners, startups, research centers, investors and citizens' initiatives on clean energy economy. This summit is global meeting point for defining industry needs and creating new business opportunities.

If your business or organisation is working to produce clean energy, increase energy efficiency, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, cut waste and pollution, or conserve water and other natural resources—you are part of the clean energy economy. This summit is design as a physical marketplace to promote your services and help businesses and organizations to network and build long-term relationships in a relaxed and productive atmosphere.

  • Date: June 21-22, 2018

Milan_Afrik Fashion Week

Presented By: Afrik Fashion International

Our theme for the inaugural Milan_Afrik Fashion Week (MAFW) is "Free to Be Me!" We are promoting great fashion and beauty for all shapes, sizes, ages, nationalities and abilities. This event will be a celebration of not only fashion, but life and culture.

MFAW event delivers a platform were the best Africa-inspired designers in fashion industry can shine. Our event includes catwalk shows, exhibitions, music, arts and business networking while promoting the works of African fashion designers and African inspired culture. Milan is Italy's financial hub and a global capital of fashion and design. Milan also known for its finest restaurants and shop. MFAW welcomes your attendance and invites all fashion designers, models, exhibitors, sponsors, volunteers and the public to participate.


  • Date: 11-12 August 2017
  • Location: Central Milan
  • Ticket Sales Begin July 1st

BirdNews Climate Alliance

Non-Govermental Organization (NGO) Initiative

BirdNews Climate Alliance (BCA) exists to push for the urgent and radical action we need to combat climate change and prevent the catastrophic destabilization of global climate.
We do this by mobilizing private and public investor to build a thriving economy in which long-term environmental and social risks are incorporated. BCA works with key players in the economy to promote clean and safe environment and also to advance sustainable solutions that will reduce pollutions, protect vital natural resources and ensure safe and just conditions for everyone affected by climate change.

Project Love: Africa

Proposed to OpenIDEO (a global community and open innovation platform) in April 2017

“Inclusion and Love for All…” We want to help create a world that is just as seamless for someone in a wheelchair to navigate as it is for a person walking on two feet. We envision a society where, no matter how extreme your size, disability, or difference, you will feel empowered to express your individuality, and won’t have to settle for fewer resources than the rest of the world. We also want the limits to come off of those who society has marginalized, and for everyone in the feel equally special.

We want to create a maker space and shop where everyone is welcome! There, we will teach community members (of any ability) how to create (design and build) and sell clothing, footwear, and product that are optimized for the specific needs of the clients who will come to the shop. We will use traditional sewing machines and fabrics as well as use 3D printing and other methods on digital fabrication to create footwear, apparel and products to meet the unique needs of our clients.
